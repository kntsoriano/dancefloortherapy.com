guidelines

  1. Dancefloor Therapy is a safe and inclusive community space meant for everyone. Homophobia, transphobia, and racism by attendees and staff will not be tlierated.
  2. Illegal and dangerous objects are not allowed. Big bags are discouraged.
  3. Smoking is only allowed in the designated smoking area.
  4. Stay hydrated. Free drinking water will be provided.
  5. If you feel unwell or see someone who needs help, please inform our staff.
  6. Bright, flashing lights and images that could cause discomfort may be used during the event. Discretion is advised.
  7. Persons exhibiting unruly behavior will be asked to leave the premises immediately.
  8. We allow taking of photos and videos provided that subjects agree with verbal consent. If you do not wish photos of you be taken, please let us know.
